Whether you are sitting on a train or waiting for a dentist's appointment, or travelling around the world, with Citibank you can take care of your banking business wherever you are! CitiPhone Banking is available to help you anywhere there is telephone service. It’s convenient and, most importantly, safe, as we draw on many years of global experience and expertise.

Starting March 1, 2016, CitiPhone Banking will be accessible 24/7 in the Interactive Voice Response (IVR) mode only. CitiPhone bankers will be available to answer your call on weekdays between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. Moscow time.

CitiPhone bankers will still be available 24/7 to assist you with any urgent matters:
  • if you have any problem using your card;
  • if you need to have your card blocked/unblocked;
  • if you need to change your PIN or TPIN;
  • if you discover that your card has been lost or stolen and detect suspicious card transactions.

Why do we have to do this?

There are many ways how banks can cut their costs. We care about our reputation of being a reliable and honest bank and have found what we believe to be the best way of cost optimization, which, although inconveniencing our customers, reduces such inconvenience to a minimum, as most of the transactions and services that our customers are in the habit of accessing via CitiPhone bankers have long been accessible using CitiPhone IVR or Citibank Online.
